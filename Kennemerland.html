Kennemerland national park

What about nature?

Kennemerland national park is a good place to see nature in Netherlands. It is a nice national park which starts in the forest and ends in dunes near North Sea. This is a large park, approximately 3800 hectares, so it is recommended to come there in the morning to have the whole day to walk through the forest and dunes. You can sometimes see different animals like deer, foxes and rabbits.

Where is Kennemerland national park located?

Kennemerland national park is located on the way to North See, so after enjoying nature you will have possibility to swim before going home. If you prefer to walk you can reach the park by foot, but it is of course easier and faster to get there by bus.
