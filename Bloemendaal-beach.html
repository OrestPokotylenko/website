Bloemendaal aan Zee beach

Swim? Sure!

If you like to swim, then visit Bloemendaal aan Zee beach. It is a nice clean beach where you could spend some time. The beach is not small, so you will surely find place even in warmest days of the year. On the beach you can also find lots of restaurants and cafes where you can get some food and rest. North Sea is a bit cold, but it is still warm enough to swim I the summer.

On the way to the sea

If you decided to visit the see it is recommended to take a bus to not be tired when you reach the sea, but if you would like to walk, it is up to you, the way to the sea is beautiful so it is not a bad idea to walk. Bloemendaal aan Zee beach is 8.5 kilometres far from Haarlem station, but you will not be lost, most of the time you will go straight forward.
